The Commercial Use Logos are for companies that have a product that contains OpenStack software or works with OpenStack software. Your company must meet the requirements and a signed contract is required.

<div class="row">
	<div class="col-md-4">
		<img src="/images/svg-logos/program-logos/openstack-compatible.svg" width="100%" />
        <p class="logo-description">If you offer compute, storage, or networking hardware or software that's designed to work within an OpenStack cloud environment, or cloud applications designed to be compatible with OpenStack clouds, you may be eligible for the "OpenStack Compatible" marketing program, which includes a specific commercial-use logo and wordmark treatment for your product name.</p>

	</div>
	<div class="col-md-4">
		<img src="/images/svg-logos/program-logos/openstack-powerd.svg" width="100%" />
        <p class="logo-description">This program is for products or services that contain OpenStack Software, such as public Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) clouds, Distributions or Appliances. These products must contain specific OpenStack code and pass interoperability tests defined at openstack.org/interop. Qualifying for this program allows you to use the OpenStack Powered logo and varying wordmark treatment for your product name, as well as potential inclusion in the OpenStack Marketplace.</p>

	</div>
	<div class="col-md-4">
		<img src="/images/svg-logos/program-logos/openstack-training.svg" width="100%" />
        <p class="logo-description">This program is for companies offering training courses focused on OpenStack. There is also a marketplace to help connect stackers with training courses in their area.</p>

	</div>	
</div>
